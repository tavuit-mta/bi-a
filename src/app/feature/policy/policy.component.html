Privacy Policy

Welcome to BilliardScore! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name and other information you provide when creating a profile or contacting support.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as game statistics, device type, and interaction logs.
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, operating system, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services and features.
  • To personalize your experience and remember your preferences.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support, or important notices.
  • To analyze usage and improve app performance and security.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your device secure.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can review and update your profile information at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@billiardscore.app.
